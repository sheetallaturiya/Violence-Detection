About Us

With the increasing number of violent incidents that are happening frequently,the safety of people is compromised.The various occurences of such incidents are captured by video survelliance techniques.The violent fights,the agreesive protests,riots and violent crowd behavior have challenged public safety.

Hence in order to provide the society with a safe evironment we propose the method with most accuracy to detect violence in video surveillance.It includes the various machine learning and deep learning algorithms to divide the video in image frames and perform analysis to detect these events on time to ensure public safety.
